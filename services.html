SERVICES & SCHEMES

Digital India Programme

The Digital India Programme aims to transform India into a digitally empowered society and knowledge economy by providing digital infrastructure, digital services, and digital literacy to citizens across the country.

e-Hospital Services

e-Hospital Services provide online access to hospital services, including appointment scheduling, patient records management, and telemedicine consultations, enhancing healthcare accessibility and efficiency.

UMANG App

The UMANG (Unified Mobile Application for New-age Governance) app offers a single platform for accessing various government services, including payments, applications, and information from multiple departments and agencies.
